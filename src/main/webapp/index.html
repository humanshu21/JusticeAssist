Practice Area

Real Estate Law

Real estate law governs the buying, selling, use, and ownership of land and property.

Insurance Law

Insurance law governs the regulation of insurance policies and claims, outlining the rights and obligations of both insurers and policyholders.

Business Law

Business law governs the legal aspects of commercial activities, including contracts, transactions, and operation of companies.

Personal Injury

Personal injury law allows individuals to seek compensation if they are harmed due to someone else's negligence or intentional actions.

Medical Neglegence

Medical negligence occurs when a healthcare professional fails to provide the standard level of care, resulting in harm to a patient.

Traffic Rule

Traffic violation law deals with offenses related to the improper operation of motor vehicles, such as speeding, running red lights, or driving under the influence (DUI).
